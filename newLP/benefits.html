benefits

The way we read is changing. We're on phones, tablets and desktops. Interactive is expected. Audience data is demanded. That's how we designed Publet. Publet is the way institutions communicate on any device whether it's with 50 staff or 5 million customers. Our team comes from the media world from Conde Nast to CNN, so we know what you need from design to publishing to data. Now it's all in one place. Use Publet to make a style guide, or as your company's platform for creating newsletters, proposals, white papers, annual reports, magazines, and more.

Simply, with Publet.

Anyone can create publications

Easy to use creation platform means non-technical staff and design and create.

  • intuitive authoring tool to create professional digital publications
  • no-training required. Start using in minutes
  • < 10% cost and time of custom development
  • keep branding and styles consistent across every publication (one-click update)

Reach phones, tablets and mobile seamlessly

HTML5 output means that your content will reflow and look great on any device.

  • deliver content through the browser for phones, tablets, desktops, integrate into your existing site or as a standalone publication with unique URL
  • always responsive to fit any screen size from wall to phone
  • offline reading options for users include send to e-reader or PDF download
  • share, and publish content on the open web

Read your audience with built-in analytics

Publet's analytics let you can track reader behavior to see how your content is being used and optimize it.

  • line-by-line analytics reveal what content catches on and what is falling short
  • test multiple versions of same publication to boost lead generation and purchases
  • understand audience desires (staff and customers) by seeing what topics are useful, confusing or shared.
  • personalize content for individual users based on profile and behavior (coming soon)
