how it works

How does Publet work? It's simple.

Set your styles

Set your styles

  • Create a (free!) style guide in Publet. Add logos, assets, fonts, and colors. All files are ready for download (no more phone calls to find the latest brand materials).
  • Future publications are already pre-populated with your styles.
  • Quickly create digital publications—all in the style of your organization—without any knowledge of HTML5/CSS.

Create content

  • Start creating in five minutes or less with Publet's intuitive authoring tool.
  • Add text, images, video, audio and even custom interactives.
  • Use our PDF parser to turn files into responsive documents you edit immediately (Pro users).
  • Manage content across your organization: maintain design consistency and empower your team to create publications.

Go everywhere

Go everywhere

  • One-click to publish HTML5 publications formatted perfectly on phones, tablets and desktops.
  • Send publications to reads in the browser as unique URLs (allow users to save as PDF or send to Kindle, Nook and other e-readers for offline reading).
  • Maintain privacy or register customers and subscribers with access and paywall settings.
  • Integrated social media sharing to spread your message.

Know your audience

  • Track who reads your content, what content they like, and how to improve it.
  • Apply A/B testing and optimization to multiply performance for lead generation, traffic and customer acquisition over PDFs.
  • Access realtime data on who actually reads your publication. Personalize it for them.
