Three Trips

by Lauren Doty and Juan Leguizamon

While the best souvenirs from trips are memories, we document our travels with pictures, videos or journals. Every time we travel overseas we immerse ourselves in the culture and explore the country. Our goal is to capture the essence of the place and share it in a narrative way. Rather than visit and pose next to typical attractions, we find our own hidden treasures and highlight them in different ways. We’re after urban legends, local eateries, secret trails and hidden gems. We play with our cameras and go on filmmaking missions, like shooting bicycles riding past timeless architecture or filming each other’s backs while strolling through town or exploring a trail. The technique enhances the experience for us, especially when locals laugh and think we’re crazy.

Juan is originally from Colombia and has spent the past 10 years living and working in San Francisco as an Art Director. Lauren has spent most of her life in California working as an Accounting Manager for various companies in the music or gaming industries. A new work opportunity for Juan transported them both to Brooklyn in 2013. While Juan works in the creative world and Lauren in business, they complement each other by exploring the outdoors and scheming grand adventures. This adventurous spirit emerged on their second date when they went on a hike ill-prepared in Point Reyes, California. Sundown came earlier than expected and they found themselves lost in the woods. They spent the night wrapped in a picnic blanket continuously clicking two empty beer bottles, hoping it would deter mountain lions in the brush. This was only the beginning of many adventures to come.
